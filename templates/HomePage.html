What is global Warming?

  • Global warming is the slow increase of global temperatures, caused by greenhouse gasses
  • Greenhouse gasses are emitted from factories and agriculture
  • These gasses reflect heat onto the earth as a greenhouse would

Facts about global warming

  • Carbon Dioxide (CO2 (common greenhouse gas)) levels have gone up by ~35 ppm since 2005
  • The temperature has gone up 1.9Of since 1880
  • Polar ice has gone down 12.85% every decade on average
  • Resulting in 427 gigatons of ice lost each year

How this will affect evolution

  • In certain areas, it will help by lengthening growing seasons
  • In other areas, it could kill vegetation in those areas
  • Certain coastal areas will be vulnerable to more hurricanes
